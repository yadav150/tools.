About Skill2Job Tools

Skill2Job Tools provides free, browser-based utilities designed for students, job seekers, and professionals across India. From age calculators to image pixel checkers, our tools help you prepare for interviews, optimize content, and boost productivity - all without collecting your personal data.

100% Private & Offline: All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. No data leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy. Tools work instantly without internet after initial load and support mobile, tablet, and desktop devices seamlessly.

Empowering Job Seekers: Built specifically for India's competitive job market, our tools help with resume optimization, interview preparation, content creation for social media, and technical assessments. Perfect for Assam, Northeast, and pan-India job aspirants preparing for government exams, banking, and private sector opportunities.
