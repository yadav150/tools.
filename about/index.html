About Skill2Job Tools

Skill2Job Tools Mission: Free online utilities designed for students, job seekers, and professionals across India. From Assam to nationwide, our tools solve real problems with zero cost, zero signup. Built for exam preparation, job applications, document management, and daily productivity with a focus on speed and simplicity.

Client-Side Technology: All processing happens in your browser using modern JavaScript libraries. No file uploads, no server storage, complete privacy guaranteed. Tools work offline after loading, support mobile devices, and deliver instant results. Optimized for slow connections and low-end devices commonly used by students and job applicants.

For Students & Job Seekers: Tools address real needs - age calculators for form filling, PDF mergers for document submission, image compressors for exam photo requirements. Focus on Assam government jobs, banking exams, SSC, UPSC and educational needs. Simple interface, no learning curve, instant usability for first-time internet users.

Created by Yadav Subba: Developed from Teok, Assam for students and job seekers nationwide. Regular updates based on user needs. Future tools planned for resume builders, exam calculators, result analyzers. Visit Skill2Job main site for job notifications and educational resources. Your success is our mission.
